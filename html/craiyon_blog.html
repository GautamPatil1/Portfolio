Craiyon:- Generate Images from Text Prompts
Gautam Patil | July, 2022
Craiyon / Dalle.mini is website based on AI model that can generate images from any text prompts!
Craiyon (previously DALL·E mini) is an AI model that can generate images from any prompt you give!
You can visit and generate your own imagination here craiyon.com




OpenAI had the first impressive model for generating images with DALL·E. Craiyon / DALL·E mini is an attempt at reproducing those results with an open-source model.
The model is trained by looking at millions of images from the internet with their associated captions. Over time, it learns how to draw an image from a text prompt. Some of the concepts are learnt from memory as it may have seen similar images.
While the capabilities of image generation models are impressive, they may also reinforce or exacerbate societal biases. Because the model was trained on unfiltered data from the Internet, it may generate images that contain harmful stereotypes. The extent and nature of the biases of the DALL·E mini model have yet to be fully documented
The model, Website is FREE!!!! You can use it without paying for anything, there are ads though to pay for the servers and the team of developers.
Event thought the Craiyon model is astonishing it does not stand up to The newer DALLE*2 AI model which uses CLIP, VQGAN etc methods to create stunning images.
